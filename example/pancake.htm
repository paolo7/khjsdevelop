How to prepare a pancake

These instructions will tell you how to make a delicious pancake.

Requirements:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Prepare the pancake mix
    • Has method: http://paolopareti.uk/dataset/simple/pancake_mix.htm
  2. Pour the mix in a hot pan
  3. Cook until golden
