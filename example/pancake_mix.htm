How to prepare a pancake mix in a bowl

These instructions will tell you how to prepare a pancake mix

Requirements:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Bowl
  • Whisk

Steps:

  1. Add the eggs in a large bowl
  2. Add the milk in the bowl
  3. Whisk the milk and the eggs in the bowl
  4. Add the flour in the bowl
  5. Whisk the mix in the bowl until it becomes sufficiently smooths
